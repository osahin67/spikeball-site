About the League

G League Spikeball is an invite‑only league featuring some of the most competitive players in the Gilroy area. Matches and tournaments are hosted at Sydney Casper Park in Gilroy, California. The league focuses on skill, competition, and community while showcasing the best Spikeball players in the region.
